Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need to book in advance? ▼

Yes, we strongly recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot. For bridal packages, we recommend booking at least 2–3 months ahead. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

We kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations within 12 hours may incur a 20% cancellation fee. Bridal package bookings require 72 hours' notice for full refunds.

Do you offer home visits for bridal makeup? ▼

Absolutely! We offer premium at-home bridal services within a 20 km radius of our studio. Travel fees may apply depending on distance. Please mention this when booking and our team will curate a bespoke package for you.

What brands do you use? ▼

We exclusively use premium, cruelty-free brands including Charlotte Tilbury, MAC, Kérastase, L'Oréal Professionnel, OPI, and several luxury Indian brands. All our skincare products are dermatologically tested and safe for sensitive skin.

Is parking available? ▼

Yes! We have complimentary parking for up to 2 hours in the building's basement. Additional parking is available on MG Road and at the nearby shopping complex. We also partner with ride-sharing apps for easy drop-off.
