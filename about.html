Our Story

We Are DemoParlour

Who We Are

Born From a Passion
for Beauty

"We believe beauty is not something you put on — it's something you reveal. Our purpose is to uncover the extraordinary in every person who walks through our doors."

Founded in 2018 by Meena Krishnan and Zara Ali, DemoParlour was born from a shared vision: to create a space where artistry, luxury, and warmth coexist in perfect harmony. What started as a small studio with two artists has grown into Kochi's most sought-after beauty destination.

Today, our team of 12 world-class beauty specialists bring together expertise from Paris, Mumbai, and London — united by one goal: making you feel like the most beautiful version of yourself.

Our Journey

Six Years of Excellence

2018

DemoParlour opens its doors in Kochi with a team of 2 artists and a dream of redefining luxury beauty in Kerala.

2020

Expanded to a full 3,000 sq ft salon & spa, launching our signature bridal packages that quickly became the most sought-after in the region.

2022

Won 'Best Luxury Beauty Salon — South India' at the National Beauty Awards. Our team grew to 10 international specialists.

2024

Launched our exclusive DemoParlour membership program, bringing 500+ loyal clients into our inner circle of beauty and privilege.
